Common Wishlist Problems

  • I put in an (Ebay, Alibaba, Wayfair, etc.) link, and it said it was invalid!

  • I put in a perfectly valid amazon link, and it still said it was invlaid!
    • Most "invalid link" errors come from a single problem with the way amazon displays it's links. When you click on a product in most contexts (such as a search, reccomended page, etc.), it displays a valid URL like so:
      Notice the "dp", which usually indicates that it's a valid link.
    • However, whenever you click on an amazon product in certain contexts related to your profile (order history, recently viewed, etc.), it displays it's link differently:
      If you see the "gp", that's a good sign your link is invalid
    • To solve this problem, copy and paste the product title into the search bar, and click on the product from there
